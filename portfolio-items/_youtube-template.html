He Is Risen! / Because He Lives

Virtual choir and orchestra project to celebrate Easter 2020 during the COVID-19 lockdown. Original idea and arrangement, Cara Thompson; video production, Daisy Lazarus.

  • Written: February 2020

If you'd like to use a variant of this arrangement in your own church, please get in touch.
